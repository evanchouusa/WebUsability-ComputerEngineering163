FLOWERS

Fiddle Leaf Fig

Fiddle Leaf Fig is a popular indoor plant featuring very large, heavily veined, violin-shaped leaves that grow upright, looking similar to flowers. They thrive in very warm and wet conditions.

Orchid

As one of the top two largest flower plant families, the Orchid consists of about 27,800 accepted species. They come in a variety of tropical colors and are typically seen in home gardens and containers.

Chrysanthemum

Chrysanthemums come in a variety of colors. With vibrant hues of white, yellow, red and pink, the Chrysanthemum features decorative pompom tops that can be beautifully displayed indoors.

Bromeliad

Bromeliads are bright red, orange, purple, or blue flowers that can grow in a number of different ways. They can be grown on the ground, growns on rocks, or grown on other plants and trees.
